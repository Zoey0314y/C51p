Interesting Facts About Camel

  1. They have a single hump on the back.
  2. There are mainly two species of true camels: the Dromedary and the Bactrian
  3. The Dromedary is found in the Middle East and is also called Arabian camel.

Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleon eyes are independent.
  • Chameleon can change their color.
  • Chameleon come in different sizes.

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons and humans have lived in close proximity for thousands of years.

Interesting Facts About Teddy-bear

  • The first British Teddy Bear Festival was held in 1989 in London.
  • The first toy bear is believed to be made by German toy maker Margarete Steiff in 1902 that was a jointed, stuffed one.
  • American multi-billionaire investor Paul Greenwood owned the largest collection of teddy bears.

Interesting Facts About Panda

  • The typical panda grows from 1.2 m to 1.9 m.
  • Pandas are native to south-central China.
  • In captivity, the average lifespan of a panda is 30 years.

Interesting Facts About Flying-Cicada

  • A cicada is a flying insect.
  • Cicadas are members of the Cicadoidea superfamily.
  • Cicadas are found throughout the U.S.
